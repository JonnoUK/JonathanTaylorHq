Lighting Designer at Exeter Northcott

Oklahoma!

In January 2019, Jonathan was the Lighting Designer for Exeter University Footlights' production of Oklahoma! In the Exeter Northcott, Jonathan oversaw the delivery of the director's vision while co-ordinating with the professional venue. The design was completed using Capture and EOS Family software, enabling an easy get-in and programming process.

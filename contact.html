Jonathan Taylor

Jonathan Taylor is a freelance theatre producer, with an eye for theatre with universally-relatable themes staged in daring and interesting ways.

Current Projects include:

The Wind of Heaven (Finborough Theatre, London) with Weighed In Productions.
The Respectable Prostitute (The Playground Theatre, London) with Lisa Forrell.
NEON (R&D) with Patrick Swain.

Previous Projects include:

The Remarkables (Greenside @ Nicholson Square, Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2019) with Shotgun Theatre.
NEON (The Lion and Unicorn Theatre, London) with Caged Bird Theatre.

As Assistant Producer:

The Claim (Roundabout @ Summerhall, Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2019) with James Quaife Productions.
Exeter Fringe Festival (Exeter Phoenix & Exeter Barnfield, 2019).
